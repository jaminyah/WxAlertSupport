App Overview

WxAlert is a mobile application that provides current weather information from the National Weather Service (NWS) for more than 20,000 cities across the continental United States. The application's primary view presents weather information that is published daily. Weather information is presented using various icons. When available, weather alert notifications, for the local area, are displayed in the primary weather view. Alert notifications are displayed using a list of icons. Details of the alert is presented, in text format, on a separate view.

Disclaimer

This application should not be used as a primary source for emergency weather information that is to be received in a timely manner. For timely information related to tornado, flash flooding, hurricane, avalanche, etc. depend on other well established sources of timely weather notification as provided by the National Weather Service and reliable media outlets.
